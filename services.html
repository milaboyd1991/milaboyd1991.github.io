01 · Interior

Interior & Flooring Consulting

Premium kitchen, bath, and remodel design. Material, light, and feel chosen with a designer's eye and ten years of field experience inside flooring showrooms, on job sites, and across high value projects.

For homeowners, contractors, and developers who want the small material decisions handled by someone who has lived inside them.

What's included
  • Material specification across flooring, tile, stone, and finishes
  • Space planning and finish schedules
  • Vendor sourcing and order coordination
  • On site walkthroughs and installation review
  • Light project management for the design phase
Best fit

Kitchen and bath remodels, full home interiors, premium new construction, and contractors who want a designer on call for client facing decisions.

02 · Product

UX / UI & Service Design

Product design that respects the user, the business, and the small decisions that compound. Research, IA, design systems, and shipped interface work. I treat a product like a remodel. Plan it once. Build it right. Maintain the system.

For founders, small product teams, and studios who want a calm partner from research through delivery.

What's included
  • User research and persona work
  • Information architecture and sitemap
  • Wireframes through high fidelity in Figma
  • Custom design system, not generic components
  • Developer handoff with documentation
Best fit

Mobile and web products at any stage. Especially strong for service businesses, home improvement platforms, and tools that bridge physical and digital experience.

Brand & Visual Systems

Identity systems, type, color, and layout that read premium on every surface. Not a logo. A complete language for a business to use. Built around restraint and the rules that make it scale without losing the feeling.

For founders ready to stop looking like everyone else in their market.

What's included
  • Brand strategy and positioning
  • Logo system, type, and color palette
  • Guidelines document the team can actually use
  • Launch assets across print, web, and social
  • One round of post launch refinement

Service businesses, hospitality, design and construction firms, and founders who want a brand that quietly says premium without trying too hard.

Honest about it

The studio takes a small number of clients per quarter so every project gets the time it deserves. Some kinds of work belong with a different partner. Saying so up front respects everyone's time.

01

Rush jobs with no planning time.

The studio runs on a calm weekly rhythm. Projects that need to ship in days work better with a different kind of partner.

02

Licensed construction outside the scope.

Designer, not general contractor. Building permits, structural engineering, and trade work belong with a licensed professional.

03

Projects without clear communication.

Decisions are made in writing during weekly check ins. A project that cannot make room for that cadence will not get the studio's best work.

04

Work that does not align with the studio's standards.

The studio reserves the right to step away from any project that compromises craft, honesty, or care for the end user.
