Case Study · UX / UI

PNW Floor Design App.

A mobile platform for smart consultation, project tracking, and home visualization. Designed for an audience that does not want a long form, just clear next steps.

Role
Product Designer
Research through wireframe
Type
Capstone UX project
Tools
Figma · HTML · CSS
Year
2026
01 · Problem

Remodeling is overwhelming.

Homeowners cannot visualize how a tile will live in their actual room, do not understand the steps involved, and end up managing communication across multiple contractors. Most existing apps cover one piece of that, not the whole journey.

02 · Solution

One guided workflow.

A platform where users visualize materials with AR, book consultations, track project timelines, manage invoices, communicate with their designer, and control compatible heated floor systems. A seamless renovation experience.

The market gap.

Three competitors, one open lane

Houzz

Large photo library and strong inspiration boards.

Too broad. No personal designer connection.

Floor & Decor

Product browsing, store locator, helpful filters.

No AR. No project tracking. No personal guidance.

Home Depot

Huge inventory and basic AR.

Impersonal. Not design focused. No scheduling.

None of these apps combine a personalized designer client workflow with smart home compatibility. PNW Floor Design fills that gap.
