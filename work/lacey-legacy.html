Case Study · Brand

Lacey Legacy. A studio identity.

A self directed brand and web identity built end to end. Name, palette, type system, logo treatment, service architecture, and a hand built web layout. Designed to test how far quiet luxury can be pushed inside restrained code.

Role
Brand Designer
Web Designer
Type

Tools

Year

01 · Brief

The brief I set.

Design a small, quiet luxury creative studio identity that could believably take on UX/UI, brand, and photography work. The site had to feel calm on first read, build trust before asking for action, and survive being built in plain HTML and CSS without losing its high end feel.

02 · Approach

I started with the feeling.

Three words shaped every decision. Warm action, luxury trust, visual novel. From those words I built the palette, picked a serif italic display face, set a generous baseline, and let space and color do most of the work. Components came last.

Design decisions.

Color, type, feeling

Palette.

A soft, dusk leaning system. Blue carries trust, peach softens the edges, pink keeps it human, sage grounds it. The ink burgundy holds everything in place. I avoided pure black. Too sharp for the warmth I wanted.

Type.

Playfair Display italic carries the personality. Source Serif Pro carries the reading. Lato handles the small mechanical work. Three voices, each with a clear job.

Design that feels like a life self portrait.

Body voice. Calm, readable, generous line height. Used for the bulk of long form copy across the site.

What I learned.

Outcome and reflection

Lacey Legacy was never going to launch as a real business. The exercise gave me something more useful than a client deliverable. A working test of how far quiet luxury thinking can go inside plain HTML and CSS, with no JavaScript framework and no premium fonts.

The biggest lesson was about restraint. Most luxury cues are subtractive. Fewer colors, fewer weights, more space, more silence. The harder skill was not designing the screens. It was deleting the parts that almost made it in.

Warm action, luxury trust, visual novel. The three threads I wove through every single decision in this brand.
