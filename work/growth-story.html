Case Study · Personal

How I rebuilt myself in a single year.

On August 7, 2025, I was fired for standing by my values. Within ten months I had started a company, sold projects, returned to school, and built a UX/UI practice. This is the design decision log of that year.

01 · The pivot

The day that clarified everything.

August 7, 2025. I was fired for standing by my values. I will not hide that. It was the most clarifying day of my life.

I had two choices. Spend the next year defending the old story, or use the time to build a new one. I picked the second one. The day after I was let go, I started writing down the principles I had been collecting for ten years inside flooring showrooms. Material discipline. Patience under pressure. The compounding power of small careful decisions. I knew there was a practice in there. I had to design it.

02 · The build

What I shipped in the first months.

This is not a hardship story. This is a build log.

Aug 7, 2025

Fired.

Walked out, drove home, opened a notebook, wrote down what would have to change. Started a study practice that same week.

Aug to Oct 2025

Built PNW Floor Design LLC.

Registered the LLC. Sold heated floors, paint, windows, and doors. Tens of thousands of dollars in projects. Partnered with contractors and suppliers. Established the business operations that the studio still runs on.

Sept 2025

Hired at Floor & Decor.

Senior Designer role at a higher salary than the job I had been fired from. Within months, the first and only designer in store history to reach 25% revenue contribution. Store average was 16 to 17%.

Oct 2025

Phi Theta Kappa.

Invited to the Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society through Pierce College. The decision to take school seriously again became the spine of the next year.

Winter 2025 to Spring 2026

Built a UX/UI practice.

Returned to school in earnest. Studied UX/UI under Min Pak. Designed the PNW Floor Design app from research through wireframes. Built a brand case study from scratch. Wrote the design system this studio runs on today.

June 17, 2026

Graduation. Full circle.

AAS in Digital Design and UX/UI certificate from Pierce College. Same date as a previous graduation in 2022. A coincidence the calendar would not let me miss.

The design principles that came out of it.

What this year taught
01

Calm under pressure.

The work that survives is the work made without panic. Calm is not a temperament. It is an output of a clear system.

02

Small decisions compound.

The same way a wrong tile choice compounds for ten years in a kitchen, a wrong type choice compounds for the life of a brand. Choose carefully.

03

Restraint reads as care.

Five colors. Two fonts. Three sizes. Restraint is what tells a viewer the designer cared enough to delete.

04

Field experience is design experience.

Ten years on the floor is not a footnote. It is the reason the system holds. The studio is built on it.
