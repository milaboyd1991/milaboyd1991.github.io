What to expect.

The first call is a calm conversation. No pressure, no pitch, no proposal yet. I want to understand the work before I tell you what I would do with it.

  • You write a brief. A few honest sentences about the project, the audience, and the timeline.
  • We schedule a thirty minute call. Either video or phone. Most prefer video. The studio is in the Pacific Northwest, scheduling around your timezone.
  • I respond within two business days. Either with a clear yes, the next step, or a thoughtful no with a recommended alternative.
