How payment works.

Plain terms
Deposit

Fifty percent up front.

A signed agreement and the first half secures the studio start date. The remainder is paid on handoff or split across milestones for longer engagements.

Methods

Card, bank, or wire.

Invoices are sent through standard accounting tools. International clients pay by wire. No crypto, no third party escrow.

Refunds

Pro rata, in writing.

If we end an engagement early, the unused portion is refunded pro rata. The studio commits the same honest accounting to itself that it commits to clients.
