Outcome & Reflection

What this case study proves.

What landed
  • Complete brand system from logo to live product
  • End-to-end ownership: brief, IA, design, prototype
  • Pricing tiers grounded in real market positioning
  • Accessibility built in, not bolted on
  • Pak's 8-section creative brief applied verbatim
What I would do differently
  • Add testimonials section once the business launches
  • Build a before/after slider with real project photos
  • Reduce competing CTA variants on early iterations earlier
  • Add a financing options block for premium tiers
  • Ship a service-area SEO page per city
