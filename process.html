Process

Four calm steps. One honest path.

Every engagement at the studio follows the same shape. A clear brief, a defined direction, careful design, a clean handoff. Whether the brief is a kitchen or a product, the rhythm holds.

01
Discover

The conversation.

A real call, a few honest questions. We talk about the audience, the constraints, the budget, the feeling the work should leave behind. I listen for the things that are not said yet.

By the end of Discover, we know what success looks like and we have written it down together. Every later decision is held against that one document.

Deliverables

  • Written project brief with success criteria
  • Stakeholder map and decision authority
  • Honest timeline and scope agreement
  • Investment range and payment schedule
02
Define

The direction.

Palette, type, structure, voice. The rules the work will follow. I show you the world the project lives inside before a single screen or schematic gets built.

Define is where we make the decisions that compound. Get this stage right and the rest of the project becomes simple execution.

Deliverables

  • Mood and tone direction
  • Color, type, and material direction
  • Layout grids and sitemap or floor plan
  • One direction chosen and signed off in writing
03
Design

The work.

Iteration in calm, careful sessions. Weekly check ins. Decisions written down so nothing gets lost in translation. I work in long focused stretches because that is where the best decisions get made.

You see real work each week. Not screenshots in a chat thread. Working drafts, real materials, things you can react to.

Deliverables

  • Weekly review sessions with written notes
  • High fidelity design files in Figma or specification documents
  • One round of refinement after the first full draft
  • A complete decision log for your records
04
Deliver

The handoff.

Final files, a small kit, and a clean hand off. I document the system so your team or contractor can keep the work consistent long after we say goodbye. Light support afterward so the work keeps its feeling.

Deliver is when the studio steps back. The work becomes yours to live with, scale, and grow.

Deliverables

  • Final assets in production ready formats
  • Mini guideline document for your team
  • Hand off call with implementation partners
  • Thirty day support window for small adjustments

How long, how much.

Honest answers up front
Timing

Six to twelve weeks.

Most engagements run between six and twelve weeks. Larger remodels and full brand systems stretch longer. Tiny scopes finish faster. I confirm the timeline in writing during Discover.

Cadence

Weekly rhythm.

One scheduled call per week and async messages between. No daily back and forth. Calm rhythm protects the quality of decisions.

Investment

From four figures.

Strategy and brand engagements begin in the low four figures. Full product or remodel work scales from there. Full pricing details on the Pricing page.
