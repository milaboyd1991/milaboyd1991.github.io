11 · Search Console setup

Get the site indexed by Google.

Five step checklist. Total time about fifteen minutes. Indexing usually begins within 1 to 7 days, full coverage in 2 to 4 weeks.

  • Add the property. Open search.google.com/search-console, click Add Property, pick URL prefix, paste https://milaboyd1991.github.io.
  • Verify ownership. Easiest method, HTML meta tag. Google gives a <meta name="google-site-verification" content="..."> string. Paste it into the <head> of index.html and push. Google confirms in a few seconds.
  • Submit sitemap.xml. In Search Console left nav, click Sitemaps. Paste https://milaboyd1991.github.io/sitemap.xml and submit. Should say Success within a minute.
  • Request indexing for the priority pages. Use the URL Inspection tool at the top, paste each URL one at a time, click Request Indexing. Priority order, Home → Services → Work → Roots → Book → Contact.
  • Check Coverage and Pages report. Wait 24 hours, then in left nav check Indexing → Pages. Look for green Indexed pages and address any errors flagged in red.
